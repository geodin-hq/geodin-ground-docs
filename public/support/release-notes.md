
# Release Notes

## v1.5.17
_Released: 18 september, 2025_  
**What's New:**
- Creating virtual logs to customize your ground model
- Adding better support for working with Civil 3D in imperial mode 

**Improvements**
- Improved accuracy when displaying and working with boreholes and ground layers, especially for complex or overlapping data.
- Smoother experience when working with boreholes at different elevations.
- Updated demo database for easier testing and exploration.
- General improvements for reliability and ease of use.

## v1.0.0

_Released: 2 june, 2025_  
**What's New:**
- Now compatible with AutoDesk Civil 3D 2026 and 2025.

**Improvements**
- More robust handling of ground data and volumes.
- Enhanced user interface for a more intuitive workflow.
- Improved error messages and feedback for a smoother experience.
- Many small improvements to make working with your data easier and more reliable.

## 0.9.0 
_Released: 17 april, 2025_  

**Features:**
- Open a GeoDin® database.
- Browses projects, boreholes, and related documents.
- Selects and draws boreholes based on input.
- Selects and draws detailed boreholes.
- Draws surfaces based on multiple boreholes.
- Creates solids between surfaces.
